Our Mission

RiseForHer is dedicated to bridging the gender gap in the IT industry through targeted tutoring, mentoring, and consulting initiatives. We empower women, advise companies, and collaborate with governments to create inclusive environments where women thrive.

Our Programs

  • Tutoring: Technical training and upskilling for women entering or advancing in IT.
  • Mentoring: One-on-one guidance from experienced industry professionals.
  • Consulting: Strategic support for companies and governments to implement gender-equity policies and initiatives.

Partner With Us

We work with forward-thinking organizations that believe in equality and inclusion. Join us in shaping a future where everyone has a seat at the tech table.
